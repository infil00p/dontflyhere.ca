Don't Fly Here

This is a map of the areas that either have an explicit ban on Recreational UAV flights, or are areas where it's highly recommended that you don't fly. This includes the following:

  • Anywhere within 9 km from an Airport, Heliport, or Aerodrome (Water Airport)
  • National Parks

This is a non-exhaustive list and is a work in progress. This is for information purposes only. Always check with a secondary resource like Transport Canada before flying.
